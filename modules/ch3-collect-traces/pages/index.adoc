= Collecting Traces from Agentic Applications

Goal::

Collect and visualize traces from agentic applications using MLflow.

This chapter covers how to use the MLflow SDK to collect traces from agentic applications.
You will learn why tracing is essential for troubleshooting agents.
You will also learn how to organize traces in experiments and runs.
Finally, you will gain hands-on experience adding MLflow tracing to your code.
