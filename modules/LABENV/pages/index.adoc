= Lab Environment

== Instructions to Launch Your Lab on the Red Hat Demo Platform (RHDP)

. Log in to the xref:#RHDP-Portal-Links[RHDP portal]
. Search for the catalog entry: `Red Hat Openshift AI 3`
. Click the catalog entry in the search results.
. On the catalog page, click menu:Order[].
. Fill out the required details in the order form.
You can use the smaller AWS instance type.
. Review the warning at the bottom of the form and check: +
menu:I confirm that I understand the above warnings[].
. Await the email notification stating that your demo environment is ready and providing its access credentails and URLs.

=== Important Notes:
- This lab may take approximately *1:30* hours to become ready.
- You can also retrieve access information directly from the RHDP portal.
- This lab uses GPU-enabled instances on AWS, which are in short supply.
It require multiple attempts to provision or restart. 

=== How to Access Your Lab via the RHDP Portal:
. On the RHDP portal, click menu:Services[] option in the left-hand menu.
. Select your lab from the services listings on the right-hand side of the page to view access details.
